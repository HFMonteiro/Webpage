Research Interests

Advancing healthcare through digital innovation and evidence-based public health strategies

My research focuses on the intersection of digital technology and healthcare delivery, with a particular emphasis on public health systems and data-driven decision making. As a PhD candidate in Health Data Science, I explore how innovative technologies can transform healthcare operations and improve patient outcomes.

Primary Research Areas

Digital Health Systems

Investigating the implementation and optimization of digital health technologies in clinical and public health settings. Focus on system integration, user experience, and measurable impact on healthcare delivery quality and efficiency.

  • Health Information Systems
  • Electronic Health Records
  • Telemedicine Platforms
  • Digital Health Workflows

Screening Programs

Developing and evaluating evidence-based screening programs for population health management. Research includes program design, implementation strategies, and effectiveness assessment using health data analytics.

  • Population Health Screening
  • Program Effectiveness
  • Health Outcomes Research
  • Risk Stratification

Health Information Systems

Exploring the design, implementation, and optimization of health information systems that support clinical decision-making and health system management. Focus on interoperability, data quality, and system usability.

  • Health Data Integration
  • System Interoperability
  • Data Quality Management
  • Clinical Decision Support

Data Visualization & Business Intelligence

Advancing the use of data visualization and business intelligence tools in healthcare to improve decision-making, operational efficiency, and health outcomes. Research includes dashboard design, analytics implementation, and user adoption.

  • Healthcare Dashboards
  • Performance Analytics
  • Visual Analytics
  • Decision Support Systems

Research Methodology

My research employs a mixed-methods approach, combining quantitative analysis of health data with qualitative assessment of system implementation and user experience. I utilize:

  • Health Data Analytics: Statistical analysis of large healthcare datasets to identify patterns and outcomes
  • Implementation Science: Systematic evaluation of technology adoption and integration processes
  • User-Centered Design: Assessment of system usability and user experience in healthcare settings
  • Health Economics: Cost-effectiveness analysis of digital health interventions
  • Policy Analysis: Evaluation of health policy implications and recommendations

Current Research Focus

WHO/Europe Consultation
