About Me

Portuguese MD/MPH and PhD candidate dedicated to transforming healthcare through digital innovation

I am a Portuguese physician with an MD and MPH, currently pursuing a PhD in Health Data Science. My work focuses on the digital transformation of healthcare policy and operations, with particular expertise in public health systems and data visualization.

Currently, I serve in multiple roles that span clinical practice, policy development, and international health initiatives. As a Public Health Physician at ULS Gaia Espinho and Programs Coordinator at Direção Executiva do SNS, I work at the intersection of healthcare delivery and strategic planning. I also serve as an External Consultant for WHO/Europe and work as an Industry Fellow and lecturer, bridging academia and practice.

My professional mission is to leverage technology and data science to improve healthcare outcomes and operational efficiency. I believe that the future of healthcare lies in the thoughtful integration of digital tools with evidence-based medical practice.

Key Impact Areas

  • Digital Health Systems: Leading implementation of innovative healthcare technologies that enhance patient care and operational efficiency
  • Public Health Policy: Developing evidence-based strategies for population health management and health system optimization
  • Data-Driven Healthcare: Implementing business intelligence and data visualization solutions to support clinical and administrative decision-making
